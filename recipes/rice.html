Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • Any type of rice 2 Cups

Steps

  1. Wash rice at least 4 times.
  2. Place rice into the rice cooker pot.
  3. Place rice cooker pot into rice cooker, plug in rice cooker and put it into cooking mode.
  4. Once it switches to keep warm mode, wait for 20 additional minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes, fluff up the rice and it is ready to serve.
