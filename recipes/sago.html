Sago

Description

Ingredients

  • Sago 3/4 Cups
  • Cubed Mango 1 Cup
  • Cut Strawberries 1 Cup
  • Coconut Jelly 1 Cup
  • Green Ai Yu Jelly 1 Cup
  • Coconut Milk 6 Cups
  • Condensed Milk 6 Tbsp

Steps

  1. Place 1.8 Liters of water and the Sago into a pot. Turn on high heat and once boiling, boil for around 10 mins.
  2. Pour coconut milk and Condensed milk into a big bowl and mix well.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well (you can separate the sago if saving for later).
